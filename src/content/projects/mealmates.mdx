## Problem
Finding healthy, customized, and affordable meal prep options locally can be difficult, while talented local chefs and meal-preppers lack a dedicated platform to reach customers.

## Approach
Built a full-stack Next.js application to act as a two-sided marketplace.
- Created vendor profiles for local meal-preppers to showcase their menus for customized meal prep boxes.
- Implemented a streamlined discovering and ordering process for users.
- Designed a mobile-responsive interface connecting local buyers and sellers.

## Outcome
A functioning local meal prep marketplace that bridges the gap between consumers looking for customized meal prep boxes and local meal-preppers looking to grow their customer base.

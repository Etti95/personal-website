## Problem
Understanding borrower behavior is critical for risk assessment in DeFi, but data is often fragmented across multiple protocols.

## Approach
Aggregated borrowing events from major lending protocols.
- Normalized data schemas across Aave, Compound, and Radiant.
- Created comparative visualizations for cross-protocol analysis.

## Outcome
Provided actionable insights into market leverage trends, helping users and analysts spot potential liquidation risks.

## Problem
Retail traders often struggle to separate noise from signal in on-chain data. Identifying "smart money" addresses that consistently outperform the market is difficult due to the sheer volume of volume and noise.

## Approach
Developed a scoring system to profile wallet conviction.
- Filtered for wallets with high retention and profitability.
- Analyzed token holding periods and accumulation patterns.
- visualized "conviction scores" to highlight high-probability setups.

## Outcome
Enabled users to follow high-signal wallets and identify accumulation phases before the broader market caught on.
